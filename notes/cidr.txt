# Validation / False Positives

Usable counts above assume network and broadcast are not assignable (/30 and wider) — /31 and /32 use point-to-point and single-host rules instead.
Scanning a large CIDR (e.g. /8) from a lab VM is not practical — expand to host list or smaller scope first.
Private ranges are non-routable on the public internet — still in scope for internal assessments.
