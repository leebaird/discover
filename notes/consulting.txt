# Backpack

Update your VMs and tools.
Power strip and extension cord.
4 port switch.
25' CAT6 cable.
(2) 6' CAT6 cables.
(2) USB-C to Ethernet adapters.


# Dress

No hats or sunglasses inside.
Shave.
Long hair slicked back in a ponytail.
Shirt — pressed button-up, one button from the top, tucked in.
Pants — pressed, belt, no jeans.
Shoes — shined.


# Set Expectations

Number of days.
Start, lunch, and stop times.
Status updates.
Deliverable.


# Know Your Environment

Location of bathroom and cafeteria.
People will be watching you AND listening.
Leave your work area clean.


# Client

Treat client with respect (yes sir, no ma'am).
Be responsive.
Control the client and the engagement — do not let them control you.
Beware of hovering.
You are not there to teach.
